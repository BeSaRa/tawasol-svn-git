Ready to Export in central archive

Ready to Export in center archive content all documents have approved before by author user , and be Ready to export from center archive .

Ready to Export in center archive content :

Serial number for every document .

Subject of the document .

Receive date the date of the receipt of the documents in the sent items .

Correspondence site the sites which the documents have been sent to .

Number of days that document are stay in ready to export .

Ready to export screen consists of several options :

Reloading : reload and upload the saved data in the screen .

Search : search for any document appears in the sent items screen using a letter or a unmber for the document .

Sort : you can perform am ascending or descending order of data.

The list of actions can taken in Redy to export in center archive :

Document info already written inside document and it is sample.

Open the document and view all general data of the document and the procedures by pressing on "open" action .

Terminate the document in case it the end of launch distribution and if need to back to this document can found it by search .

Export the document from center archive to outside entity and can chosse if Export ( Attachment - linked document) with document or not .

Print Barcode used in the document .

Return Document and write comment and reson .

View tracking sheet with all history of the document and you can Export or print this history .

Manage documents can add or update (Tags - Comments - Linked documents - attachments - Linked entities ) .

Download  document the main document or composite document with attachments and linked documents .

Duplicate current or specific verision of any document you need and have option to edit on it after duplicated .
