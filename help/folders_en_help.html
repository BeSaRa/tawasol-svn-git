Folders

The purpose of the user folders is to transfer the mail from your mail to your own folders in order to sort

      them out of the correspondence in the mail until the date of the procedure, as in the following figure:

 

 

 

Window content:

Serial: The icon that appears in documents.

Subject: The subject of the document.

Date of receipt: The date of receipt of the documents Sent items.

Action: Action on documents submitted in terms of approval, review and rejection.

Sender: Responsible for sending the document.

End date: The date of completion of the document.

Correspondence Site: The entities sent to them.

Type: The type of Sent items

 

The system allows you to perform actions on documents directly from the folder, and allows you to return

       them to your inbox by clicking the "Return to Inbox" button.

You can create your own folders through the "User Options" section of the "Create Folders" section.
