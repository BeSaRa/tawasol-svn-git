Favorite Documents

The system allows you to add favorite documents to you for viewing at any time through the

      " Favorites Documents" window, where the preferred documents appear as follows:

 

 

Window content:

Document Subject: The subject of the document.

Document Type: The type of the documents at favorite documents.

Security Level: the document security level.

Priority Level: A priority of document within your favorite documents.

Document Date: The date of the document.

Serial: The icon that appears in documents.
