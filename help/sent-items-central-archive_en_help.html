Sent Items in Center Archive

Tawasol System allows you the sent items screen within the Center Archive list to follow up all the procedusers on the documents.

Sent items in Center Archive Content :

Serial number for every document have sent before .

Subject of all documents .

Type of the document ( Original or Copy ) .

Sender the user how send this document .

Main Site from that the main site where the document received from .

Main site to the  main site which the docunent sent to .

Sub site from that the sub site where the document received from .

Sub site to the sub site which the document sent to .

Receive Date the date of the receipt of the documents in the sent items .

Status of the document after sent (print and Terminate - received - ....).

Sent items screen consists of several options :

Reloading : reload and upload the saved data in the screen.

Search : Search for any document appears in the sent items screen using letter or number for the document .

Sort : you can perform an ascending or descending order of data .

Date : can modifiy month and year to find the srnt items .

The list of actins can taken in sent items page :

Document info already written inside document and it is sample .

Open the document and view all general data of the document and the procedures by pressing on "open" action .

Launch New Distribution Workflow in case need to resend this document .

View tracking sheet with all history of the document and you can export or print this history .

Manage documents can add or update (Tags - Comments - Linked documents - attachments - linked entities ).

Download document the main document or composite document with attachments and linked documents .

Send the document by mail the main document or composite document with attachment and linked documents .

Get Link makes you open the document outside the system by prees on the link display when take " Get link" action .
