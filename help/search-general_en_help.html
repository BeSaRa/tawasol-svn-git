General Search

Search for any document and memo saved electronically easily .

Can search by one item or more in the same time .

The following points are the basic indexing data that you can search by :

Select organization , can choose between all the OUs in the system (according to your permissions) (Optional).

Document format Electronic or Paper (Optional) .

Security Level of the issued document you are looking for , either (Normal , Confidential , Private , Secret , Top Secret) (Optional).

Priorty Level of the document you need , either (Normal, Urgent, Top Urgent) (Optional) .

Document subject full or part thereof (Optional) .

Year of the document you need (2019- 2018- ...) (Mandatory) .

The date of the document (From - To) automatically change depends on the choosen year (Optional) .

Main classfication of the document (Optional).

Sub classfication of the document and it depends on the main classfication (Optional) .

Serial number of the document you search for (Optional) .

Document Type (Optional) .

Followup Date (From- To) .

File name (Optional) .

File Code (Optional) .

File number (Optional) .

Notes if therefor (Optional) .

The Following points are the advanced data that you can search by :

Linked Entity linked inside the document (Optional) .

Serial Number (From - To) (Optional).

After The selected search has been approved , click the " Search" button at the end of the search results page .

The system lets you take actions on documents displayed in search results by selecting the desires document and then clicking the desired action (according to your terms of reference depending on the nature of the document .
