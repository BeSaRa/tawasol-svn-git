Document Stamps

Through the new viewer feature, the Administrator can add digital stamps for departments and Sections in the Tawasol system through the “Document Stamps” screen that is included in the Administrator's list

Administrator can add a stamp in Tawasol after selecting a required organization unit through the screen shown above, more than one stamp can be added to the same organization unit, and the Administrator can enable or disable the stamp, the stamp can be deleted if it is not used anymore in any document.

Administrator can add the stamp through the organizational structure screen after modifying the required department and adding the stamp to it
