Review -Internal Mail

  The system allows to you to review the internal document which have been entered in you administration and take the action based on the authenticity of the entered documents .

  When click on Review option from Internal Mail , the system opens the Review screen to display all the internal document electronic and paper .

  Review screen contains the following procedures which user can take any of them in accordance to the permission granted to him :

  Accept   : accept the document to send it directly to Ready to send screen

    Accept and launch distribution workflow : accept and launch the document to the user which will be selected from Send screen

  Reject  : reject the document from the Review screen

  Broadcast : broadcast the internal paper document only to multiple organizations and groups from Broadcast screen

  Notes :
