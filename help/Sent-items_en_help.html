Sent Items

The System allows you the sent items screen within the inbox list to follow up all the procedures on the documents .

Sent items content :

Serial number for every document .

Subject of the document .

Receive Date the date of the receipt of the documents in the sent items .

Action taken on the sent document when sent or transfered to another user .

Due Date of the document .

Correspondence Site the sites which the documents have been sent to .

Sent Items scrren consists of several options :

Reloding : reload and upload the saved data in the screen .

Search : Search for any document appears n the sent items screen using aletter or anumber for the document .

Sort : you can perform an ascending or descending order of data .

The list of actions can taken in sent items page :

Recalled document in case the document does not opend from user inbox or taken any action on the document by pressing the right click and select "Recall" action with the recall reason .

Open the document and view all the general data of the document and the procedures by pressing on "open" action .

  Get Link makes you open the document outside the system by prees on the link`display when take "Get Link" action .

Duplicate Specific version of any copy of version you want to duplicate .

Manage documents can add or update ( Tags- Comments- Linked documents- attachments- linked entities ) .

Send the document by mail the main document or composite document with attachments and linked documents .

View tracking sheet with all history of the document .

Download document the main document or composite document with attachments and linked documents .
