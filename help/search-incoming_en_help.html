Incoming Search

Search for any incoming document saved electronically easily .

Can search by one item or more in the same time .

The following points are the basic indexing data that you can search by :

Select organization , can choose between all the OUs in the system (according to your permissions) (Optional) .

Security Level of the issued document you are looking for , either (Normal, Confidential, Private, Secret, Top Secret) (Optional) .

Priority Level of the document you need , either (Normal, Urgent, Top Urgent) (Optional) .

Document number of the incoming document (Optional) .

Document subject full or part thereof (Optional) .

Year of the incoming document you need (2019- 2018-...) (Mandatory) .

The date of the incoming document (From - To) automatically change depends on the choosen year (Optional) .

Main classfication of the document (Optional) .

Sub classfication of the document and it depends on main classfication (Optional) .

Serial number of the incoming document you search for (Optional) .

Document type (Optional) .

The date of the document (From - To) (Optional) .

Followup Status (Need Reply - Without Reply - Replied) (Optional) .

Followup date (From - To) (Optional) .

Notes if therefor (Optional) .

The following points are the Advanced data that you can search by :

Main site you can make it active or deactive (Optional) .

Correspondence site type (Optional) .

Linked Entity linked inside the incoming document (Optional) .

Serial number (From - To) (Optional) .

After the selectedsearch has been approved , click the "Search" button at the end of the search window.

 

If the system finds what matches the search you selected , it displays on the search results page .

The system lets you take action on documents displayed in search results by selection the desires document and then clicking the desired action (according to your terms of reference depending on the nature of the document ) .
