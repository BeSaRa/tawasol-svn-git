Outgoing and Incoming Search

Search for any outgoing and incoming Document saved electronically easily .

Can search by one item or more in the same time .

The following points are the basic indexing data that you can search by :

Select organization ,can choose between all the OUs in the system (according to your permission) (Optional) .

Security Level of the issued document you are looking for , either (Normal, Confidential, Private, Secret, Top Secret) (Optional).

Priority Level of the document you need ,either (Normal, Urgent, Top Urgent ) (Optional) .

Document Subject Full or part thereof (Optional) .

Year of the document you need (2019-2018-...) (Mandatory) .

The date of the document (From - To) automatically change depends on the main classfication (Optional) .

Serial number of the incoming and outgoing document you search for (Optional) .

Main classfication of the document (Optional) .

Sub classfication of the document and it depends on the main classfication (Optional) .

Serial number of the incoming and outgoing document you search for (Optional) .

Document type (Optional) .

Followup status ( Need reply, Without reply, Replied) (Optional) .

  Followup date (From - To) (Optional) .

File name (Optional) .

  File code (Optional) .

Notes written inside document (Optional) .

The Following points are the advanced data that you can search by :

Main Site you can make it active or deactive .

Correspondence site Type (Optional) .

Linked Entity linked inside the outgoing document (Optional) .

Serial Number (From - To) (Optional) .

After the selected search has been approve , click the "Search" button at the end of the search window .

If the system finds what matches the search you selected , it displays on the search results page .

The System lets you take action on documents displayed in search results by selecting the desires document and then clicking the desired action ( according to your terms of reference depending on the natur of the document) .
