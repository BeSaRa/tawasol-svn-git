Sent G2G

Shows all outgoing documents that are exported in the current month (by default) to other government

      entities by government correspondence.

To view sent items during any other month, Month and year can be selected using dropdown at the top of the page.

 

 

Actions: Displays last action done via Government correspondence.


There is “delivery report” to view all actions. If “delivery report” is selected, all actions done using Government correspondence are

        displayed like Sent, Returned, and Resend.

  Action list shows Action, Destination details, Date and time of action, Email address and comments.


After document is received by the other entity, Receiver name and time is saved in Actions.

 

Delivery Report:

 

 

 

Status: Shows the status of the document.



Waiting – Outgoing document is exported and in Cloud but not reached final destination yet.


Sent - Outgoing document has reached final destination successfully.


Returned – Outgoing document is returned back by destination government entity


Recalled – Outgoing document can be recalled after sending before the destination government entity has received the document


Received: The destination has received the document successfully.


Outgoing document information include Subject, Type of Document, Security Level, Document number, Government Reference number, date, Sender department.
