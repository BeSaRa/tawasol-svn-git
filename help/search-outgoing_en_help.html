Outgoing Search

Search for any outgoing document saved electronically easily.

Can search by one item or more in the same time.

The following Points are the basic indexing data that you can search by :

Select organization , can choose between all the OUs in the System (according to your permissions ) (optional).

Document format Electronic or Paper (Optional) .

Security level of the issued document you are looking for , either (Normal, Confidential, Private, Secret, Top Secret) (Optional) .

Priority Level of the document you need , either (Normal, Urgent, Top Urgent) (Optional) .

Year of the document you need (2019- 2018- ... ) (Mandatory) .

The date of the document (From - To) automatically change depends on the choosen year (Optional).

Document subject full or part thereof (Optional) .

Main classficition of the document (Optional) .

Sub classficition of the document and it depends on the main classfication (Optional) .

Serial number of the outgoing document you search for (Optional) .

The following points are the Advanced data that you can search by :

Main site You can make it active or deactive (Optional) .

Correspondence site Type (Optional) .

Linked Entity linked inside the outgoing document (Optional) .

Approver the user how sign the document (Optional) .

Serial Number (From - To ) (Optional) .

After the selected search has been approved , click the "Search" button at the end of the search window .

If the system finds what matches the search you selected , it displays on the search results page .

The system lets you take action on documents displayed in search results by selecting the desires document and then clicking the desired action (according to your terms of reference depending on the nature of the document .
