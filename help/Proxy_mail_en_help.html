Proxy Mail

The system allows you to connect to proxy mail through the proxy mail inbox window. This window only

       works after a user assigns you as an assistant. It consists of the following:

 

 

Window content:

Serial: The icon that appears in documents.
Subject: The subject of the document.
Date of receipt: The date of receipt of the documents Sent items.
Action: Action on documents submitted in terms of approval, review and rejection.
Sender: Responsible for sending the document.
End date: The date of completion of the document.
Correspondence Site: The entities sent to them.
Type: The type of Sent items.

 


As shown, the Proxy mail inbox Window consists of a list of options divided by terms:


Reload: Reload and upload the saved data on the system.
Select User: You can choose a user who has identified an associate where they can be the client for more than one user.
Search: Search and find a specific file.
Current Page: Current page number.
Number of page elements: The number of documents on the page.
Navigating between pages: You can navigate between pages (next - previous).
Sort: You can perform an ascending or descending sort of data.
