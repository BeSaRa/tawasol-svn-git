Simple add Internal Mail

  Simple add is a quick way to add internal document electronic or paper with the possibility to display the document properties and the content of the document in the same screen .

  When add a new simple internal document user should fill all the mandatory fields .

  Notes :

  In case you add an internal document paper or electronic without content , the document will be saved in Prepare screen

  When user add internal electronic document from simple add you can save it as draft when click on "Save As draft" button and the document will be saved in Draft screen .
